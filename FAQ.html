...Freaquently Asked Questions...


How is the Covid 19 outbreak effecting you?

We have a robust Business Contingency Plan in place to help us continue to support both students and staff during this unsettling time. We are working in partnership with Wokingham and other councils to ensure we are taking all possible precautions whilst also supporting other organisations to ensure that those with learning disabilities are supported in our community. We have closed the office to all visitors but are on the phone to offer assistance as required.


How do we get started?

Just give us a call or send us a message online to arrange a call back to discuss your needs. After talking with you on the phone, we will carry out an assessment to determine your support requirements – either at your home or our office in Wokingham. We offer a flexible bespoke service so you can choose how you would like to meet your support worker(s). Our aim is to create an established team of regular support workers, so you’ll always know who’s coming.


What’s the minimum support time you provide?

We do a minimum of 1 hour calls.


How will my support be organised?

You will have a dedicated coordinator and dependent on the needs of your service you may also have a Lead Support Worker to manage your team of support workers.


Which geographical area do you cover?

We operate mainly in Wokingham, Bracknell and Reading.
